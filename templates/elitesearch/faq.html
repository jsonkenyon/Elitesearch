Frequently Asked Questions

How does this site make money?

Simply put, we don't make anything by running this website. We are just a group of passionate individuals; that like our privacy just as much as you do. If we ever feel that we need to make money to support this server, we will ask for donations. Though at this time there are no plans to do this and we are running this service out of our own pockets!

There really isn't any logs?

Yep, that is correct. You can see in our GitHub repo in the Caddyfile the logs are just discarded. This isn't going to change and we will never enable them.

What about website cookies?

SearXNG does use a cookie for the "preferences" interface, as there is no login model. When using this interface and making local changes, you accept the usage and storage of this browser cookie on your computer.

Is Elite Search Opensource?

You bet, we have nothing to hide and we feel that other people that may be running SearXNG might want to take a look at our templates or even use them themselves, with their logo of course. Which is all fine by us, if you like our design and how we customized the look of searXNG, please head over to our GitHub repo and fork it and make it your own!
